EDUCATION

School can be very overwhelming so it is important to get into a daily routine. Personally, I wake up and cook my breakfast. Then, I go to my classes. Next, I come back and start preparing my lunch and workout while it's cooking. Then, I either go to more classes or do homework. Last, I cook dinner and do my nightly routine.

Above is a photo of my class schedule for this semester. My hardest class is definitely EECS 281 and my favorite class is SI 339. With such a crazy schedule it is very important to keep a calendar or planner to keep track of everything. I like to keep a calendar with my schedule with my class on my computer so I can also view it on my phone. I write down everything I have to do in a planner and check things off throughout the day. I love my planner because if I don't write down all my assignments I sometimes forget to do something. I recommend making a new page in your planner for each day to keep things organized.
